Summary of chapter 1
-HTML pages are text documents
-HTML use tags, which are characters that sit inside angled brackets. They act as containers and tell you something 
	about the information that lies within them
-Tags are usually referred to as elements
-Tags usually come in pairs. The openning tag denotes the start of a piece of content; the closing tag denotes the end
-Attributes require a name and a value
-To learn HTML you need to know what tags are available for you, what they do and where they can go.
